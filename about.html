Founded in Lagos with a passion for transforming the real estate experience, EstateVita has grown to become one of Nigeria's most trusted property platforms — connecting families with spaces they truly call home.

Who We Are

A Decade of Trusted Real Estate

EstateVita was born from a simple belief — that finding a home should be an exciting, transparent, and rewarding experience, not a stressful one. Since 2014, we have helped over 850 families and individuals across Nigeria navigate the real estate market with clarity and confidence.

From luxury villas in Ikoyi to modern apartments on Victoria Island, we curate only the finest properties and pair them with expert guidance — ensuring every client feels supported from first search to final handover.
